Ford GoBike System Data Exploration
¶

by Karma Dolma Gurung
¶



This presentation showcases summary of exploratory analysis of 1.3 million bike trips taken with Ford GoBike System from Jan 2020 to Jun 2020 covering the greater San Francisco Bay area.

Conclusion¶

  • Weekdays see more usage than on Weekends.
  • 5pm sees the highest usage of the bike.
  • Saturdays and Sundays on average take longer time for the same distance compared to Weekdays.
  • Subscribers take lesser time to travel the same distance compared to Customers.
